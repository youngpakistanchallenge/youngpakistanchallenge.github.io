Mentors & Speakers

YPC speakers, mentors and jury are some of the most distinguished individuals in their field of work. They come from extremely diverse backgrounds ranging from business, culture, social development, media, publishing, academics, startups, personal growth and many more. The idea is to connect you with inspiring individuals who can connect with your passion and choice of career, who offer support and encourage youth to develop their skills, improve their performance and become the person they want to be.

Why Become a Mentor? Are you in control of your life? Do you know where you are heading and the steps needed to achieve your goals? Mentoring allows you to focus on how you can forward your career. A mentor can provide guidance, support and space for you to think.

How To Become Mentor?

Are you ready to share your knowledge and experience of following your passion as a mentor or a speaker in YPC?

If you would like to be a mentor / speaker, please write to us at info [at] youngpakistanchallenge.org mentioning more details about yourself. That will help us to get to know you better and see if your profile fits into our mentorsâ€™ criteria.

Before becoming a mentor, here are a few things to understand about the role of mentoring. Most of us have had a teacher, supervisor, or coach who has been a mentor to us and made a positive difference in our lives. Those people wore many hats, acting as delegators, role models, policy enforcers, advocates, and friends. Mentors assume these different roles during the course of a relationship, and share some basic qualities like a sincere desire to be involved with a young person, respect for young people, have active listening skills, have ability to see solutions and opportunities and have flexibility.

CV, LinkedIn profile, Sample of your work, achievements: website, blog, social media, articles, videos etc, Details of an organization/company you are associated with, Experience in mentoring (if any) and in which area of specialization
