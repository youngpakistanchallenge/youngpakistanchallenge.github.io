About Us

ABOUT YPC

Pakistan is among countries with the largest youth populations in the world. Youth comprise around 63% of the country’s population, meaning three out of every five Pakistanis fall in the youth category, while 15 per cent of the youth is unemployed.

The majority has neither discovered its human potential due to low quality education (or none at all), nor can it participate in the process of the cultural, social and economic development of the country.

Young Pakistan Challenge is an invite-only transformational event that gathers the top young minds from across the country to innovate, connect and challenge themselves where you energize your leadership growth, empower your creative vision. This tribe comprises of people who want to take action to turn their passion into a successful career. We are dedicated to providing multiple services through our nine core components. These services will enrich the student's experiences during the program enhance future opportunities for them to succeed as productive citizens. We encourage positive change in Pakistani Youth.

About YPC

MENTORS & SPEAKERS

YPC speakers, mentors and jury are some of the most distinguished individuals in their field of work. They come from extremely diverse backgrounds ranging from business, culture, social development, media, publishing, academics, startups, personal growth and many more. The idea is to connect you with inspiring individuals who can connect with your passion and choice of career, who offer support and encourage youth to develop their skills, improve their performance and become the person they want to be.

Why Become a Mentor? Are you in control of your life? Do you know where you are heading and the steps needed to achieve your goals? Mentoring allows you to focus on how you can forward your career. A mentor can provide guidance, support and space for you to think.
