Youth Challenge Award

YOUTH CHALLENGE AWARD

In our community, there are youth who deserve some special recognition for their significant contribution in the lives of others.

They are the ones that stand out from their peers. They have a quality that makes you take notice. You feel you are in the presence of someone special. They have an entrepreneurial spirit and are positive leaders. They volunteer their time, they have faced incredible odds and come out on top. They are passionately working to make this world a better place. These youth make a significant contribution in the lives of others.

AWARDS/ CEREMONY

All nominees, their families and nominators will be invited to the awards ceremony to be held at Young Pakistan Challenge event. Each nominee will receive a commemorative certificate. One award winner in each of the 5 categories will be selected, by a committee appointed by the Youth Challenge Awards steering committee, to receive Rs.25,000/ and a plaque contributed by Young Pakistan Challenge. Selection will be based on the persuasiveness of the information provided by the nominator.

QUALIFICATION

Individuals or groups must be a University Student (Graduate/Masters) and either live in any part of the country. The achievement must be completed or ongoing in the year prior to October 31, 2018.

SELECTION PROCESS

One award winner in each of the 5 categories will be selected by a committee appointed by the Youth Challenge Awards steering committee. Selection will be based on the persuasiveness of the information provided by the nominator. The committee may contact the nominator for further information.

NOTE: An award may be withheld if the selection committee considers there is no qualified candidate.

YCA CATEGORIES

COMMUNITY SERVICE

Helping communities achieve their full potential. Taking an active role in creating a better community through service.

COURAGE

Handling a difficult situation such as standing up to peers to maintain high ethical standards, or ignoring personal safety.

CREATIVITY

The ability to generate or recognize ideas, alternatives, or possibilities that may be useful in solving problems.

LEADERSHIP / INSPIRATION

Energizing and creating a sense of direction and purpose for individuals and excitement and momentum for change.

GROUP AWARD

Achievement by any group age between 18-30 in any one of the above four categories.
