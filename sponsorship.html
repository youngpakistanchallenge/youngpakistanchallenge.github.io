REQUEST FOR SPONSORSHIP

To,
                 The Brand Department
Subject:   Request for Sponsorship for Young Pakistan Challenge

Dear Sir,

              As a valuable member of our community, we would like to invite your company to be a sponsor of our Young Pakistan Challenge (YPC). Young Pakistan Challenge is an opportunity where young people (male and female) can better prepare for their future. YPC is an opportunity to discover and explore what you love doing by taking up an incredible challenge and connecting with the best mentors and career opportunities.

Pakistan is among countries with the largest youth populations in the world. Youth comprise around 63% of the country’s population, meaning three out of every five Pakistanis fall in the youth category, while 15 per cent of the youth is unemployed. The majority has neither discovered its human potential due to low quality education (or none at all), nor can it participate in the process of the cultural, social and economic development of the country.

What could possibly be the reason for the youngest country in the world to not fully utilize its greatest resource – the human potential of its people? Young Pakistan Challenge is an opportunity to challenge yourself and others in the quest of finding some answers and following your true passion. We are looking for you to be part of Yong Pakistan Challenge to make this event truly special for our youth of the country. Your involvement in this event would bring a unique and memorable experience.

Thank you for your consideration and I look forwards to the opportunity to speak with you personally in further detail about this exciting opportunity. Detail proposal and program details are attached with this letter.

Best Regards,

Riaz Ahmed
Executive Director YPC
